Lets Travel to Pakistan

Traveling in Pakistan by air is convenient, with a well-established domestic airline network connecting major cities and remote regions. Airlines like Pakistan International Airlines (PIA) and private carriers offer comfortable journeys, perfect for exploring popular destinations and off-the-beaten-path gems. From bustling cities to breathtaking mountains, flying allows efficient exploration of Pakistan's diverse landscapes. Enjoy convenience, professional service, and awe-inspiring vistas during your air travel adventures in Pakistan.

1: Pakistan International Airlines


Pakistan International Airlines was established on 23rd October 1946 as Orient Airways. PIA operates scheduled flights to 23 domestic destinations and 30 international destinations in 27 countries across Asia, Europe and North America. PIA Booking Office or the main hubs are in Karachi, Lahore and Islamabad/Rawalpindi. Secondary hubs include Peshawar, Faisalabad, Quetta, Sialkot and Multan.

2: Airblue


Airblue is a leading airline in Pakistan, known for its excellent service and affordable fares. Established in 2003, the airline commenced its commercial operations in 2004. Airblue operates scheduled flights connecting six domestic destinations (Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Rahim Yar Khan, Sialkot, and Multan) with international destinations including airports in the UAE (Dubai, Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi), Muscat (Oman), Jeddah, Riyadh, and recently Dammam. class="line-button">

3: Askari Aviation


Askari Aviation is a prominent aviation company in Pakistan, based in Punjab, Rawalpindi. Established in 1995, it has participated in numerous mountain rescue missions, including K2. The company enjoys support from Pakistan Army Aviation and provides international charter services to support peacekeeping troops deployed worldwide. Their services include air ticketing, ground handling, and flight training for various roles in traffic, flying, cabin crew, and cargo services.

4: Shaheen Airlines


Shaheen Airlines is a private carrier in Pakistan, operating in major cities of Pakistan and the Gulf. It began operations in 1994, initially focusing on nationwide flights.
