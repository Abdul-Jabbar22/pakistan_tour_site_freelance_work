3D View of Badshahi Mosque

The Badshahi Mosque in Lahore, Pakistan, is a true masterpiece of architectural grandeur and spiritual significance. This awe-inspiring edifice stands as a testament to the magnificence of Mughal architecture and the enduring legacy of the Mughal Empire. With its intricate design, imposing minarets, and vast courtyard, the Badshahi Mosque exudes a sense of timelessness and serenity. The 3D model of the mosque beautifully captures its architectural elements, allowing viewers to appreciate its grandeur from various angles.


3D View of Faisal Mosque

The Faisal Mosque, an emblem of modern Islamic architecture, comes to life in an exquisite 3D model that captures its graceful grandeur and spiritual significance. This meticulously crafted model pays homage to the iconic mosque that stands as a symbol of Pakistan's faith and unity. The intricate details of the 3D rendition replicate the mosque's unique and contemporary design. The model showcases the mosque's distinctive sloping roofs, inspired by Bedouin tents, which seamlessly blend with the landscape and the sprawling Margalla Hills. The minarets rise majestically, framing the central prayer hall like sentinels of devotion, their sleek lines and elegant proportions accentuating the mosque's minimalistic beauty
